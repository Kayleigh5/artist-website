Bio

Kayleigh Beard (1993, The Netherlands) started producing electronic music in 2015, just before starting her MADtech master's degree in art en technology. Since a young age Kayleigh has always been recording music, singing, playing guitar and keys, and performing in bands. On the side she pursued a career of computer programming and webdevelopment. During the MADtech master her passion for technology and music finally blended, and being in an art environment gave a unique touch to her music. On a personal level, Kayleigh is intensely passionate about our planet and all life forms on it. She prefers to spend most of her time outdoors, in her (vegetable) garden or swimming in a lake. So it's no surprise that elements from nature come back into Kayleigh's music and words. Her debut album "Ocean's Pulse" (July 2020) was inspired by the heartbeat of the earth and the passion to take good care of it. On stage Kayleigh performs as a one-woman-band playing electronic music with firm beats and dreamy vocals, which can be described with words such as indie electronic, deep bass, chillstep, triphop, melancholic, ethereal.
